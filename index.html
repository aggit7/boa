At Boat Owners Assoc, we're more than just an association;
we're a passionate community dedicated to making the boating experience better for America's recreational boaters.
As the nation's largest recreational boating advocacy, services, and safety group, we take pride in our role as advocates, service providers, and promoters of safe and enjoyable boating.

 

Our Mission:

Boat Owners Assoc is driven by a mission to enhance the recreational boating lifestyle for our members. We achieve this by providing a comprehensive suite of benefits, advocating for boaters' rights, and offering substantial savings opportunities. With a membership we're a powerful force committed to making boating better.
